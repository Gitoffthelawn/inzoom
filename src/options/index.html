Tests area

Hover mouse over the video or image, press the modifier key(s) and use mouse wheel to zoom in or out.
Optionally, click and drag to position the element.

Configuration

Zoom Modifiers

Wheel

Dragging

That is, after you zoom in/out an element, this element becomes "draggable", so you can move it around in its container

Context menu

Showing default - useful on Chrome/Opera and sites overriding default context menu (like YouTube on video elements).

Bringing to front

In case an image is covered by another element, you can press this shortcut, while hovering over it, to bring it to the front.

Alternative zooming: zoom in and bring to front

In case an image is covered by another element, you can press this shortcut, while hovering over it, to both zoom in and bring it to the front.
