Tests area

Hover mouse over the video or image, press the modifier key(s) and use mouse wheel to zoom in or out.
Optionally, click and drag to position the element.

Configuration

Zoom Modifiers

Wheel

Dragging

That is, after you zoom in/out an element, this element becomes "draggable", so you can move it around in its container

Context menu

Useful on Chrome/Opera and sites overriding default context menu (like YouTube on video elements).


Help

In case an image is covered by another element, you can press Ctrl+Shift+], while hovering over it, to bring it to the front.

To reset an image to its oryginal size/position, you can press escape key, while hovering over it.
